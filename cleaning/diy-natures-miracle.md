## Halved DIY Pet Stain and Odor Remover Recipe

Ingredients:

* 2 cups (16 oz) of 3% hydrogen peroxide
* 1-2 tablespoons of baking soda
* Â½ to 1 teaspoon of liquid dish soap

### Instructions:

1. Combine the ingredients in a standard 16oz (2-cup) spray bottle.
2. Gently swirl the bottle to mix the ingredients; do not shake vigorously.
3. Use immediately as directed in the previous instructions.

### Safety Reminder:
This solution must be used immediately and cannot be stored in a sealed container due to gas build-up from the chemical reaction. 
Perform a spot test on an inconspicuous area of your carpet or upholstery before full application to check for colorfastness.
